ABOUT COMPANY

Trusted Aluminium & Glass Specialists

Butt Aluminium is one of Pakistan's trusted aluminium and glass solution providers, delivering premium-quality products for residential, commercial and industrial projects.

Our expertise includes aluminium windows, aluminium doors, curtain wall systems, spider glazing, ACP cladding, frameless glass, office partitions and customized architectural glass solutions.

With years of experience, skilled professionals and high-quality imported hardware, we focus on delivering projects that combine strength, elegance and durability.
